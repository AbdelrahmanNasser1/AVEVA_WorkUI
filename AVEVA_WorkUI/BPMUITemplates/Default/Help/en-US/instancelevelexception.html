Instance Level Exception

You can view the instance level exceptions from the BAM reports.

  1. Go to Enterprise Console menu, and then click Reports.
    The Business Activity Monitoring page appears.
  2. Click Analyze, click Transaction, and then click Failed.
    Details of All Workflows Executed with Errors appear on the right pane.
  3. Double-click an item to open.
  4. Click the View Exceptions tab to see all the exceptions that has occurred for the particular workflow instance.
