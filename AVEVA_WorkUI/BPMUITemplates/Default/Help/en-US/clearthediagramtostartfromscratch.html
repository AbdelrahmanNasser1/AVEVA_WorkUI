Clearing the Process Diagram

In AVEVA Work Tasks you can clear the entire process diagram. You do not have to delete the diagram activity-by-activity. This is very useful when you are modeling the process. You may have to redo the process diagram many times until you arrive at the correct process model.

To clear an existing process design:

  1. From the File menu, select the Clear Diagram option.

     
  2. Proceed to create a fresh process diagram from scratch.
