Using Approval Wizard in Workflow

Scenario:

Consider a simple scenario called Loan process. An employee logs into their company HR Site and Applies for Loan. The employee has to select for a particular type of loan. In case the employee selects car loan from application goes to Car Loan Wizard. In case employee applies for two wheeler loan application goes to two wheeler loan wizard.

Go to the Context menu, click the Wizards and select the template in which you need and use it in the workflow.
