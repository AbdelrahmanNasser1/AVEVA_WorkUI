Different Email Template for Different Activity in a Workflow

Each Human activity like Approval, Choice, Checklist and Task Activity can have its own email template that will be sent to an actor.

Example:

Let us assume that a workflow has two Approval activities and the mail format sent from Approval Activity1 is different from that of Approval Activity2.

To achieve the same, AVEVA Work Tasks introduced a category Mail Channel in the Activity Properties of the Human activity, to have different formats for different activities.
