Process Modeling and Saving the Analyst's View

The Process Designer comes with a modeling layer over the process design layer. In the earlier versions, the Process Designer could only be used to design a process definition for automation. The new Process Modeler/Designer, with its visually-rich user-interface like Visio – a tool familiar to Business Analysts – allows them to easily model business processes. In addition, the BPMN activities like Lanes and Pools allow better modeling.

With these new features you can try out various approaches to model the business process and arrive at the optimum model. At this point, you can save the model to a file. This file can be set as the Business Analyst's model, which will be shown in the Process Execution View window when you click the Analyst's View tab.

Alternatively, you can save a copy of the completed process design as the Analyst's View and remove the automation activities, retaining only the business model.
